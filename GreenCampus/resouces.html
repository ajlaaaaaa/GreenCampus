Eco Tips

Learn easy ways to lessen your influence on the environment, such as reducing the amount of plastic you use, eating more plants, using reusable water bottles, and using mass transit. For students who are new to sustainability and want to get involved but aren't sure how to get started, these simple suggestions are ideal.

Smart Shopping

Learn how to make wiser purchasing decisions. You can minimize waste, buy in bulk, purchase locally, and select items with little packaging or made from recycled materials. These sensible purchases support the ideals of ethical and sustainable principles.

Green Transport

Get advice on how to move around your campus in a sustainable way. This includes cycling, walking, using public transport, or carpooling. This helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions, decrease traffic congestion, and lessen your carbon footprint.
