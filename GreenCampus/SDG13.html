SDG 13-CLIMATE ACTION

Act Now for a Greener Tomorrow - Support SDG 13

Sustainable Development Goal 13 focuses on urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. GreenCampus supports this goal by encouraging eco-friendly habits, second-hand selling, and awareness among students.

What is SDG 13?

  1. Climate change is affecting every country
  2. SDG 13 urges everyone to reduce emissions, adapt to climate impacts, and educate others
  3. Your actions - like selling used items instead of throwing them away

How GreenCampus Supports SDG 13

  1. Second-hand Marketplace: Reduces waste and production emissions
  2. Sustainability Tips: Daily actions students can take to live green
  3. Community Forum: Share eco ideas, join clean-up drives, and promote climate awareness.
  4. Educational Resources: Learn how to reduce your carbon footprint
