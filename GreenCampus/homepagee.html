Our Dedication to SDG 13: Addressing Climate Change

Supporting SDG 13: Acting For Our Planet

GreenCampus supports the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 13 by promoting climate-conscious habits among students. Through second-hand selling, eco-education, and community action, we aim to reduce carbon footprints and create a greener future—starting on campus.
