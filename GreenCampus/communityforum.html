Sustainability Living Tips

Dorm Room Sustainability : 5 Easy Ways to Go Green in Your Dorm Room

  1. LED bulbs are more energy- efficient and have a longer lifespan than conventional ones.
  2. Devices should be unplugged while not in use to prevent "phantom energy" depletion.
  3. When doing small loads of laundry, use a drying rack rather than the dryer.
  4. Maintain a little indoor plant; it improves the atmosphere and air quality !
